# Active vs Passive Voice

In active voice, the subject performs the action. In passive voice, the subject receives the action. Active voice is generally clearer and more direct.

## Key Rules

1. **Prefer Active Voice** — Active sentences are shorter, clearer, and more engaging.
2. **When Passive Works** — Use passive voice when the doer is unknown, unimportant, or deliberately obscured.
3. **Identify Passive** — Look for a form of *to be* + past participle (*was written, is being built*).

✅ The researcher conducted the experiment.
❌ The experiment was conducted by the researcher.

✅ Someone stole my bike. (active)
✅ My bike was stolen. (passive — doer unknown, acceptable)

✅ The committee made the decision.
❌ The decision was made by the committee.
