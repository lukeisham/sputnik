# Conciseness

Concise writing eliminates unnecessary words without sacrificing meaning. Every word should earn its place.

## Key Rules

1. **Cut Filler Words** — Remove *very, really, actually, basically, just, that* when they add no meaning.
2. **Avoid Redundancy** — Don't say the same thing twice (*past history, advance planning, each and every*).
3. **Replace Phrases with Words** — *Due to the fact that → because; in order to → to; at this point in time → now*.
4. **Use Strong Verbs** — *Make a decision → decide; conduct an investigation → investigate*.

✅ We must finish the report today.
❌ It is important that we make sure we finish the report at some point today.

✅ Because it rained, the game was cancelled.
❌ Due to the fact that it was raining, the game was cancelled.

✅ She decided to leave early.
❌ She made the decision to leave early.
